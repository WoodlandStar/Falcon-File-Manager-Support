Frequently asked questions

Where are my files stored?

On your device, inside the app. There is no cloud sync and no account. If you have iCloud Backup turned on, your data is included in your normal device backup, by your choice.

How do I lock a folder?

Long-press a folder and choose Lock Folder, then set a passcode. You can also unlock with Face ID or Touch ID.

I deleted something by mistake. Can I get it back?

Yes, when Trash is enabled. Deleted items move to the Trash, where you can restore them. You can open the Trash from Settings or the bin icon.

Which video and audio formats are supported?

A wide range, including MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI, FLV and WMV for video, and MP3, M4A, AAC, FLAC, WAV and OGG for audio.

How do I move files from my computer?

Turn on WiFi Transfer in Settings, then open the address it shows in a web browser on a computer connected to the same WiFi network.

How do I play a video on my TV?

Open a video, tap the cast button, and choose your device. Falcon supports AirPlay, Screen Mirroring, Google Cast and DLNA.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

In Settings, tap Restore Purchases. Your Pro unlock is tied to your Apple ID and restores for free on any device.

Does the app collect my data?

No. Falcon does not track you, show ads, or send your files anywhere. Everything stays on your device.
